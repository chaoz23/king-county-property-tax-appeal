# Disclaimers (surface to the owner, briefly)

- **Not legal or tax advice.** This skill is decision-support and document preparation, not legal
  representation or a licensed appraisal. For a high-value dispute or an appeal beyond the county
  board, advise consulting an attorney or a licensed appraiser.
- **Estimates until validated.** Assessed values, characteristics, comps, and the effective rate are
  pulled from public data and should be confirmed against the owner's actual valuation notice and the
  certified tax roll before filing. Savings figures are ballpark.
- **Owner owns the filing.** The property owner is responsible for the accuracy of everything
  submitted and for meeting the deadline. Missing the deadline forecloses appeal for the cycle.
- **Pay the existing bill while pending.** An appeal does not pause the tax due; pay on schedule to
  avoid penalties. A win produces a corrected bill or a refund.
- **No outcome guarantee.** Roughly a quarter of appealed values receive some reduction; a defensible
  packet improves odds but guarantees nothing.
